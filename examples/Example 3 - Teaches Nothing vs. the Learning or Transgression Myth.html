Minimal Vignette

Scene
A compact Eden reading is presented as if the scene teaches that humanity begins through learning, transgression, guilt, sexed blame, or the origin of Reciprocity Loss. A bounded PMS-EDEN reading instead treats Eden as a late minimal test scene that receives an already-developed grammar: coordinated Ω, boundary, breach, consequence activation, exposure shift, legibility management, and R trace without theological, moral, sexed, or historical origin claim.
Temporal Boundary
From the moment the Eden scene is introduced as a learning/transgression origin to the moment the reading is tested against PMS-EDEN's demotion of Eden into demonstration.
Surface Description
The scene may initially appear to be a lesson about knowledge, sin, transgression, guilt, sexuality, or a founding Fall.
EDEN Question
Does the Eden scene found or teach the EDEN architecture, or does it only demonstrate an already-developed grammar under strict non-moral and non-origin boundaries?

  • Separates consequence activation from knowledge-origin, guilt-origin, and sex-truth readings.
